Classic Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

A Simple Childhood Classic

Every day after school I would come home and make myself a snack. More often than not that snack would be a peanut butter and jelly sandwich. I've fried them, eaten them with the crusts cut off, made them three layers high; regardless of how they're made, they're always delicious!

Ingredients:

  • Bread of your choice
  • Peanut butter, creamy or chunky
  • Jelly, strawberry, grape, apricot, etc

Directions:

  1. Lay out two slices of bread
  2. Spread peanut butter on one piece of bread
  3. Spread jelly on other piece of bread
  4. Put slices of bread with peanut butter and jelly together
  5. Cut the sandwich in half if you like or eat it whole!
